RMB bonds offer investors relatively stable value in a volatile world (21 December 2012)

At the moment, global uncertainty is creating an investment environment which is both risk-averse and ‘searching for yield’. Although many fixed income instruments in the developed world can offer security, the record low yields, and anaemic growth, do not make for a compelling investment story. On the other hand, higher-yielding emerging market debt has the downside of increased volatility and higher credit risk. However, a portfolio of investment grade RMB bonds can offer investors the relatively better security of investment-grade quality bonds combined with a chance to benefit from the currency’s potential gradual appreciation and a relatively attractive yield. This diversity can protect investors from global volatility while also allowing for capital growth over time.More >>
